About Me

My name is Pedro. I graduated from the University of Central Florida in 2013 with a degree in Medical Laboratory Sciences. I discovered the beauty of web programming while in college and made my first website before graduating. At that point I started to second guess my decision for the major I chose. I love clinical science and to this day still enjoy what I do, but deep down I've always have had a passion for computers.

The first website that I made was not exactly programming, more of a drag and drop "what you see is what you get" type of setup made with Adobe Muse, but it's the experience that opened up my mind to the possibilities. Soon after, I started to teach myself HTML, CSS, and Javascript. After months of online tutorials, and endless google searches of how-tos I finally started to feel like I understood what was going on. I came across JQuery, and felt very comfortable from the start.

With my new knowledge of JQuery I designed a mobile application using JQuery Mobile and CocoonJS called ClikWik. Using the Wikipedia API the mobile app is used to look up Wikipedia articles, parse the HTML then display the article as clickable fragments that could be stored using the browsers IndexedDB API. The user interface was not perfect, but the experience taught me the process of uploading a mobile application to the Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and Amazon App Store.

A few months after uploading my first mobile application, I started to teach myself AngularJS. Soon after I discovered Ionic and decided to re-write my mobile application. In the process I also discovered Firebase and decided to use this to store data over the web.

The second time around was a major improvement to my first attempt at making a mobile application, and the experience opend my eyes to even more possibilities. With these new skills I built a web appilcation for a relative's business, a prototype for a web-based POS system, and a prototype for a web-based time tracking application.

My goal from all of this is to enter the field of professionsl web development. I am eager to learn new skills and bring my own to a team.
