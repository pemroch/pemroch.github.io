About Me

My name is Pedro. I graduated from the University of Central Florida in 2013 with a degree in Medical Laboratory Sciences. I discovered the beauty of web programming while in college and made my first website before graduating. At that point I started to second guess my decision for the major I chose. I love science and to this day still enjoy what I do, but deep down I've always have had a passion for computers and how they function.

The first website that I made was not exactly programming, more of a drag and drop "what you see is what you get" type of setup made with Adobe Muse, but it's that experience that opened up my mind to the possibilities. Soon after, I started to teach myself HTML, CSS, and Javascript. After a months of online tutorials, and endless google searches of how-tos I finally started to feel like I actually understood what was going on. I came across JQuery, and felt very comfortable from the start.

With my new knowledge of JQuery I designed a mobile application using JQuery mobile and CocoonJS called ClikWik. Using the Wikipedia API the mobile app was used to look up Wikipedia articles, parse the HTML then display the article as clickable fragments that could be stored using the browsers IndexedDB API.The user interface was not perfect, but the experience taught me the process of uploading a mobile application to both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

A few months after uploading my first mobile application, I started to teach myself AngularJS. Soon after I discovered IonicJS and decided to re-write my mobile application using Ionic. In the process I also discovered Firebase and decided to use this to store data over the web.
